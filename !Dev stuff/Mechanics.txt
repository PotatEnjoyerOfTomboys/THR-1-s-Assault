

Starting a run
	Pick between Lord's or Curtis' party
	Pick each teammate's weapon for the fist 5 missions

Playing during a run
	Get modifiers
	Pick 1 of 3 mission
		after the first mission, the player can visit the shop
		on mission 5 and 10 pick between 2 boss missions
		on mission 15, the player has to fight 1 of 2 final bosses
			if using Lord's party
				Curtis alone if the player took more then X
				True final boss if the player took less then x
			if using Curtis' party
				Lord's Party if the player took more then X
				True final boss if the player took less then x

	Pick who goes out on the mission
	Find mission money reward

	Play the mission

	Gain money
	Teammates who didn't go on the mission get healed
	Everybody gets their ammo back
	After mission 5 and 10
		Pick each teammate's weapon for the next 5 missions

Shop
	The player can
		Buy upgrades randomly choosen from a pool. 
			Upgrades stay in the shop until bought or after a boss mission. 
				Then they are replaced by a diffrent upgrade randomly choosen from the pool.
		Heal injured characters
			This is not cost effective.



The money reward for finishing mission stay constant through a run but modifiers gives chances to make more if you take risks. Modifiers are more common as the run goes on
