THR-1's Assault - 0.3


	Default controls
Input		Action

WASD		Move
E		Interact
R		Reload
Space		Dash
Left mouse	Shoot
Right mouse	Alt fire
Z		Give order: Hold	Select all teammates
X		Give order: Follow	Select teammate 1
C		Give order: Attack	Select teammate 2
V		Give order: Act Freely	Select teammate 1
Escape		Enter pause menu
PageUp		Screenshot

	New Stuff
Character upgrades for THR-1 characters. Zoar Colonists will have their own for the next version. As of now there's around 80 upgrades.
The Shop is now working, you can use it to buy upgrades, heal your party and switch current weapons.
New weapons, each character has 2 weapon that can be unlocked. 
	A character unlocks their second weapon option after surviving mission 5 and their third after mission 10
1 new boss, mission 10 now uses the Attack Helicopter as it's boss instead of the Hover Tank. More bosses will be added for the next update.
During the month of October, playable characters will have alternate outfits. I am considering adding more alternate outfits later on.
